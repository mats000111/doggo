Call for help!

The stray dogs in thailand struggle daily and we need your help! Together we can provide them a better life and bright future. We go around daily to feed the dogs and provide medial attention where needed. Educate the locals on how to deal with the stray problem and when enough funds are available we neuter as much dogs as possible. Helps us with your donations and support!

About us

My name is Mats and I am the founder of DOGGO. Ever since moving to Thailand I felt pity for the situation of the stray dogs here and decided to dedicate my time in providing a better life and future for them.
